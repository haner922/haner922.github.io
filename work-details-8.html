Mobile hair salon

Presenting a new user experience of choosing hair styles and salons
UX, GUI | 2017

Role: UX strategy, wireframe and task flow, GUI interaction design, and concept movie












Concept

Molly is a mobile application service that offers choice of hairstyle and price















User Painpoint

Many users of hair salon have uncomfortable experience everytime they visit the salon. For some, it's the unpredictable price for the service, and for some it's short time to select style.

People choose salons for their cost or for a specific designer that works at the salon. Molly provides optimal hair dressing experience for the users by transparency in cost and evaluation of designers.
